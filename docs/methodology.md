# Project Methodology

## 1. Data Preparation
The source dataset was reviewed and organized in Microsoft Excel. To improve analysis readiness and support dynamic reporting, the raw dataset was converted into an Excel Table. Helper fields were also created to support time-based reporting, including year ordered, month ordered, month number, and year delivered.

## 2. Descriptive Analytics Design
Four PivotTable and PivotChart combinations were built to answer the required business questions:

- Sales by distribution center, year ordered, and customer type
- Sales by product category across month and year
- Average delivery time by distribution center and year
- Total sales by drink flavor

Each visualization was placed on its own worksheet and paired with slicers for interactive filtering.

## 3. Dashboard Construction
A final dashboard worksheet was designed to combine all four visualizations into one screen. The dashboard used a dark executive-style background, consistent spacing, aligned chart cards, and connected slicers to support real-time filtering. The design goal was to create a layout that was both professional and easy to interpret.

## 4. Interactivity
Slicers were used to allow users to filter the dashboard dynamically by key business dimensions such as:
- year ordered
- year delivered
- month ordered
- distribution center
- flavor
- category
- customer type

This allowed the dashboard to function as an interactive operations reporting tool rather than a static workbook.

## 5. Business Insight Potential
The final dashboard supports operational review by helping users:
- compare total sales across regions
- identify category sales patterns over time
- evaluate demand by flavor
- monitor delivery performance across locations

## 6. Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- PivotTables
- PivotCharts
- Slicers
- Worksheet and dashboard formatting tools

## 7. Outcome
The final result is a clean, interactive Excel dashboard that demonstrates practical descriptive analytics skills and dashboard design for business reporting.
